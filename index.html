How it works

One Click To Clarity

Sign in with Gmail. SuperDuper does the rest. Seriously.

Your calendar, updated

"Rehearsal scheduled for 4PM. It's your week to bring snack." SuperDuper sorts through the mess of your inbox, finds the relevant information, and shows you your kids' week.

To-do list, wrangled

"Buy New Tap Shoes For Carlo." No more dropped balls (or tight cleats) again. SuperDuper builds your to-do list and does it all while you focus on literally anything else.

"Summer Debate Club Registration Now Open." Stop learning about the perfect camp three weeks too late. SuperDuper knows your family and your kids' preferences and keeps an eye out for new cool stuff so you don't have to.
