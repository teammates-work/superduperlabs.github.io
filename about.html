Who We Are

We Built SuperDuper Because We Needed It.

The SuperDuper team has kids. They play 6 instruments across 4 bands. They are on more softball teams than we can count. They do basketball, soccer, volleyball, ultimate frisbee, capoeira, curling, gymnastics, dance, theater, robotics. One has a paper route. They're in French Club and Drone Flying Club. One is learning to drive. Two are preparing for Bar Mitzvahs.

We've sent them in home jerseys to away games. We've waited in the carpool line when we forgot it was a late start at school. We've searched through 14 different email threads from five different senders about a single Scout trip and still got the drop off wrong.

We built SuperDuper because we were tired of all the work on top of the work of actually raising kids.

We're not trying to automate parenting. We're trying to give back the hours that logistics steal. The ones spent scrolling, forwarding, and mentally rehearsing tomorrow at 11pm when we just want to parent, to teach, to play.

We built SuperDuper to work for all families. Same-sex, single parents, grandparents who do pickups, and nannies who need the whole picture. We built it for co-parents splitting weeks and couples splitting sanity. We built it for us. Because in almost every household, someone is carrying a weight the other person can't fully see. And that person deserves more than a "have you tried a shared Google Calendar?"

A lot of people think about the feeling that a user gets when they open an app that they rely on. We love that. But, we're not building SuperDuper for that moment. We're building it for the feeling you get when you close the app. We're building for the moment when you already know what's next. And you can go be with your kids.

Our Vision

Software that figures out what you need.

No prompts. No configuration. Just connect your data and let the app build itself around your life.

What We're Building

We call them adaptive applications.

Not vibe coding.

We don't ask users to describe what they want. The app observes your existing data and assembles the right experience automatically.

Not just agents.

Agents act on your behalf. Adaptive applications understand what matters, organize it, and stay current without being told. Then they can act.

Not one-size-fits-all.

Every user gets a different app. Same infrastructure, same codebase, but a completely personal experience shaped by their data.

Shared infrastructure. Personal everything else.

SuperDuper is a platform for building applications that adapt to each user. We handle the hard parts — data ingestion, entity resolution, real-time updates — so every app on the platform can deliver a deeply personalized experience from the moment you connect.
